Welcome to Smart Software Store

is a leading engineering systems solution provider offering clients with solutions to a range of Software, electronic, IT and Security systems. In addition to its services, the company offers services specializes in Security, Fire, BMS, and AV Systems, and is a major contender in the engineering and systems integration market.

We provide software applications to support next generation supply chains where everything is interconnected from manufacturing through to in-store shopper marketing. We constantly survey the market, introduce new technologies and enhance functionalities, to help our customers getting further business insights and benefit most out of the system.
